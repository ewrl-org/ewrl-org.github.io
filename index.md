EWRL has now been established as a non-profit association in Europe. To ensure a stable organisation of future events, we would like to call for help with the organisation at several levels. 

## Call for organisers: EWRL 2027 with a joint venue for RLC Europe 2027

Dear colleagues,

To strengthen the connections between the **Reinforcement Learning Conference (RLC)** and the **European RL community**, **EWRL is partnering with RLC to offer a joint venue in 2027**.

Concretely, this means that **EWRL will expand its usual format** to host authors of accepted RLC papers and to accommodate both **local keynote speakers** and **joint (broadcasted) RLC keynotes**.

As organisers, you would have some freedom on how you plan to integrate both conferences together. EWRL could have:

- A dedicated day prior to or after RLC  
- Specific times in the schedule allocated for each program  
- Special keynote sessions within the shared schedule  

There would be **two locations for RLC in 2027**:
- One in the **United States**
- One in **Europe co-located with EWRL**

For this special edition, we are seeking a **motivated organising team capable of hosting a significantly larger event**—one that can welcome the growing EWRL community as well as a portion of the RLC program. We estimate a total attendance of **approximately 450–600 participants**.

In recent years, **EWRL has attracted around 200–250 attendees**, with a strong representation of students. Traditionally, the **local organising team has also taken on program-chair responsibilities** (selecting and inviting keynote speakers, handling submissions, etc.), though these tasks can be distributed flexibly.

For **2027**, you would be listed as **Local Chairs for RLC**. You would also coordinate with **RLC Program Chairs and the General Chair** to ensure that the joint program elements (e.g., poster sessions, broadcasted talks) are scheduled **without overlapping the local program**.

We are excited at the prospect of **bringing RLC to Europe for the first time through this partnership**, and we look forward to receiving your proposals.

---

### Proposal Guidelines

Please submit a **2–3 page document** [to us](mailto:european.reinforcement.learning@gmail.com) addressing the following points.

#### Proposed Host Location

- Where would you host **EWRL + RLC 2027**?
- Provide details on **accessibility for international travelers**, including:
  - Transport connections
  - Nearby airports
  - Accommodation options
- Mention any relevant **institutional support**, **local organisational help**, or **existing financial backing**.

#### Venue Capacity

- Can you confirm availability of a **venue capable of hosting ~600 attendees**?
- The venue should ideally include:
  - A **plenary room**
  - **Breakout spaces**, if needed

#### Legal and Administrative Support

- Do you have a **local legal entity** able to manage:
  - Venue contracts
  - Registrations
  - Payments

> If not, this is **not a blocker**—support can be provided. Please specify your situation.

#### Organising Team Structure

Please outline the **planned team roles** (only provide names when available—we understand the event is in **~1.5 years** and things may change).

Suggested roles include: Venue logistics,  Program chairs, Website and Communications (we will also have help at the EWRL level). 


---

We look forward to collaborating with an **enthusiastic team** to make **EWRL + RLC 2027** a **landmark event for the European RL community**.

Please send your proposals to [european.reinforcement.learning@gmail.com](mailto:european.reinforcement.learning@gmail.com) **with the title preamble [EWRL 2027]**.
